Call of Duty: Zombies is a popular game mode that debuted in Call of Duty: World at War (2008) and has since become a staple of the franchise. It features a cooperative survival gameplay experience, where players face off against waves of increasingly difficult zombie enemies. Over the years, the Zombies mode has expanded into its own unique storyline and lore, featuring distinct maps, characters, and settings.

gameplay generally involves players fending off zombies while completing objectives, unlocking areas, and upgrading weapons. One of the key elements of the mode is the "perks" system, where players can drink special drinks that grant them abilities, like quicker reloads or more health. Players can also earn points by killing zombies, which they use to open doors, buy weapons, and upgrade equipment.

Key Features:

play:

Up to four players can join a match to fight zombies together.

Perks:

Various perks like Juggernog (more health)

and Speed Cola (faster reload) help players survive longer.

Easter eggs and storyline:

Each Zombies map often has hidden quests (known as Easter eggs) that tie into a larger, mysterious storyline.

Map Design:

Maps have different themes, from a World War II bunker to futuristic or even apocalyptic settings.

The mode has evolved with every new Call of Duty release, introducing new maps, features, and characters. Some players are especially drawn to its deep lore, involving secret organizations, time travel, and apocalyptic events.
