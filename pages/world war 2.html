Zombies Introduction:

WWII Zombies focused on horror and atmosphere, with the first map, "The Final Reich", offering a darker, more intense experience than previous versions.

Storyline:

Set during World War II, players face off against Nazi zombies created through dark science and occult rituals. The story is more structured and cinematic.

Gameplay:

Core features like the mystery box, perk-like upgrades, and boarding windows return, alongside new enemy types, complex Easter eggs, and the Pack-a-Punch machine.
